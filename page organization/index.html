YF7 Tournaments

"YF7 Tournaments" is a French organization which organizes Matcherino tournaments every week, all hosted by YF7 which is Matcherino Partner. It's a project that started at the beginning of July 2025 with an idea from YF7 proposed to Gandtzmann, so they worked throughout the summer until today to ensure that everything was organized, and perfect during tournaments, on the discord server or even for the image of the organization. This organization aims to become the largest Matcherino tournament organization and to please the Brawl Stars EMEA community who run our tournaments. For now all our tournaments are in EMEA regions, but we are coming to other regions soon! Thank you for being there and thank you for your trust
