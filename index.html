    About Us

    Meet the team behind YF7 Tournaments

    YF7

    YF7 is a French streamer specializing in Brawl Stars, known for his strategic gameplay and infectious energy. On his Twitch channel, he offers dynamic gaming sessions, tips for improving, and fun moments shared with his loyal community. Beyond streaming, YF7 is actively involved in organizing tournaments on Matcherino, contributing to the competitive Brawl Stars scene.

    Nartoxy

    Nartoxy is an essential member of the team, recognized as the main investor in our tournaments. Thanks to his financial and logistical support, he enables our events to grow and become more professional. In addition to his role as an investor, Nartoxy is actively involved in the organization and management of the tournaments, contributing to their smooth execution and to the participants’ experience.

    Gandtzprime

    GandtzPrime is a former Twitch streamer specializing in Brawl Stars, known for his deep knowledge of the game and competitive spirit. Today, he serves as the lead tournament leader, coordinating and overseeing the organization of our events. With his experience in the streaming scene and expertise in tournament management, GandtzPrime ensures the smooth running of competitions and maintains a high standard of quality. A true driving force behind the project, he combines passion and professionalism to grow the community and enhance the quality of our tournaments.

    IKOZZ

    Ikozz is a key member of the staff, known for his role as an admin and his exemplary commitment to the team. Thanks to his sense of organization and his thoroughness, he ensures the smooth running of events and the coordination between different staff members. Always available and attentive, Ikozz provides quick and effective solutions, guaranteeing an optimal experience for all participants. His professionalism and dedication make him an essential pillar in the success of our tournaments.

    Our moderators ensure every day that our tournaments run smoothly and that community standards are respected. Thanks to them, organization on Discord and Matcherino remains smooth, fair, and enjoyable for all participants. Always attentive, they answer your questions, resolve disputes, and make sure each competition takes place in the best possible conditions.
