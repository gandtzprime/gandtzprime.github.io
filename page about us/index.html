About Us

Meet the team behind YF7 Tournaments

YF7

YF7 has been a Twitch Streamer since 2022, which allowed him to become a Supercell Creator in 2024! He is a French Caster and also Partner Matcherino! At the beginning of July 2025, he decided with the help of Gandtzmann, Ikozz and Nartoxy to create a series of tournaments called "YF7 Tournaments" and since then, this organization has continued to evolve thanks to you!

GANDTZPRIME

Gandtzmann is a Twitch streamer and CEO at "YF7 Tournaments", he was there from the start of the project in early July 2025, he is also the web developer and designer! He continues to do what he loves while helping the "YF7 Tournaments" project to develop and to be present as Lead Tournaments and Matcherino Support of our tournaments!

IKOZZ

Ikozz has been a member of the YF7 Tournaments organization since its inception, and also helped with even older projects. He contributed to the majority of the early tournaments and helped develop the organization both internally and externally through tournament administration and partnerships. He is fully committed to everything he undertakes, which is why he is now CEO.

NARTOXY

Nartoxy is an Investor and Administrator at "YF7 Tournaments" and just like Gandtzmann and Ikozz he has been there since the start of the project! You can find it on the discord server if you need any questions!
